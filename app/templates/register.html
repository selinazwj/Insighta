Privacy & Data Agreement

Please read and agree to our data practices before creating your account.

What we collect

We collect your email address and encrypted password to create your account. After registration, you can add optional profile details such as background, location, education, and study preferences from your Profile page.

How we use your data

Your profile information is used exclusively to match you with relevant research studies on Insighta. We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

Research participation

When you participate in a study, researchers can see that a participant matching their criteria completed their survey — but they cannot identify you personally.

Data security

Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text. We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit and at rest.

Your rights

You can update or delete your profile information at any time. You may request full account deletion by contacting us, and we will remove your data within 30 days.
