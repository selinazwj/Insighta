How it works

Two sides. One community.

Whether you're running a study or contributing to one, Insighta makes the whole process effortless — for everyone in the room.

For researchers
Students, academics, founders
1
Publish your study
Describe your research and set filters. Use AI to fill in the basics from a single sentence — who you need, how long, what you'll offer.
2
We match you automatically
Insighta surfaces your study to participants who fit. No cold outreach, no awkward group chat posts, no begging.
3
Collect and export
Watch responses come in live. Download your data whenever you're ready. Close the study when you hit your target.
For participants
Anyone who wants to contribute and earn
1
Build your profile once
Tell us about yourself — your field, habits, experiences. We use this to match you to studies that actually fit your background.
2
See your matched studies
Every study you see is a personal match. Check the reward, time, and fit score before committing to anything.
3
Participate and get paid
Complete the study. Earnings go straight to your account. Withdraw to your bank at any time — no minimum wait.
Why Insighta

Research belongs
to everyone.

We built Insighta because great research kept getting stuck — not for lack of ideas, but lack of access to real, diverse people.

Privacy by design
We never sell participant data. Your profile is used only for matching — never shared with researchers without your consent.
Your time is valued
Every study shows time commitment and reward upfront. No surprises. Participants always know exactly what they're signing up for.
Inclusive by default
We actively recruit diverse participants. More voices in research means better research — for everyone, not just the loudest rooms.
Real impact
Studies span mental health, climate attitudes, education equity, and more. Your participation helps research that actually matters.
Transparent payouts
Earnings visible in your dashboard from day one. Withdraw to your bank any time. No hidden fees, no minimums, no waiting games.
Real support
Submit feedback anytime from your dashboard and earn credit. We're a small team that actually reads everything — not a faceless platform.
