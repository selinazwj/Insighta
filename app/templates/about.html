About Insighta

A clearer path from a research question to the right participants.

Insighta is a two-sided research recruitment platform. Researchers can publish surveys or interviews, define who they need, and manage participation. Participants can discover studies, review the commitment and reward, and decide which opportunities fit them.

How the platform works

Built for both sides of research participation

The platform keeps study discovery, eligibility details, participation tracking, response review, and reward status connected in one workflow.

1

Researchers publish

Create a survey or interview listing, describe the target audience, specify the time and reward, and publish a shareable study page.

2

Participants evaluate

Browse available studies or receive profile-based matches, then review the eligibility criteria and participation details before starting.

3

Progress stays visible

Researchers monitor responses and participants can track study and reward status through their respective dashboards.

Product principles

Useful information before commitment

A study page should make the decision to participate easier, not obscure the trade-offs.

Transparent listings

Study pages surface the format, expected duration, target audience, available spots, and reward information provided by the researcher.

Participant choice

A profile may be used for matching, but a participant chooses whether to join each study and can review the listing first.

Researcher responsibility

Researchers remain responsible for ethical review, informed consent, lawful data collection, and the accuracy of their study materials.
